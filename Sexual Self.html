Sexual Self

This section explores my sexual identity, desires, and how I understand and express my sexuality.

Based on our previous topic about the Sexual self, my sexual self is about how I understand myself, connect with others, and handle relationships and intimacy, based on six main ideas. First, it's about who I am sexually and my gender (SOGIESC). Second, I believe in sharing power fairly in relationships and respecting boundaries. Third, I value feeling emotionally close, safe, and committed in love. Fourth, I think monogamy is the best for lifelong love, but I'm open to other agreed-upon relationship types. Fifth, trust comes from clear talks about what's okay, and cheating breaks those agreements. Sixth, I take responsibility for my health and my partner's health. All these parts together guide how I express myself, build relationships, and understand intimacy.
