Political Self

This section explores my understanding of governance, my civic awareness, and my engagement with political processes and societal issues.

Based on our previous topic about Political Self, my political self is my evolving understanding of how society works and is governed and is shaped by experiences from family discussions and school elections up to observing national debates on issues like government funds. These events, along with media exposure, have taught me about political processes, the importance of transparency in the government and the impact of decisions on people's lives. As a citizen, I see my role as being informed and engaged and using my values to guide my political choices towards fairness and accountability, believing that active participation can positively shape society.
